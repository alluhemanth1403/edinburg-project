

## Scottish Mammal Observations — Frontend Prototype

A React-based visual prototype of the Scottish Mammal Observations website with sample data, suitable as a reference for your Figma design and coursework.

### Pages

1. **Home/Landing Page** — Hero section about Scottish mammal biodiversity, featured species cards, quick stats (total species, total observations)

2. **Species Listing Page** — Grid/list of Scottish mammals (red squirrel, pine marten, otter, red deer, wildcat, etc.) with thumbnail images, name, and observation count. Includes search/filter functionality.

3. **Species Detail Page** — Detailed view for a selected species: description, habitat info, conservation status, observation history table, and a simple chart showing observations over time.

4. **Submit Observation Page** — Form to log a new sighting: species selector, date, location, notes, photo upload placeholder.

5. **Map View (Enhancement)** — A page showing observation locations on a placeholder map layout.

### Design & UX
- Nature-inspired color scheme (greens, earth tones)
- Responsive layout (mobile + desktop)
- Clear navigation header with links to all pages
- Accessible typography and contrast
- Placeholder species images via public domain wildlife icons
- Sample hardcoded data for ~8 Scottish mammal species

### Data
- All data will be hardcoded/mock (no backend) — species info, sample observations, stats
- Recharts for the observation trend chart on species detail pages
